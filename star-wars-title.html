Episode XXII: The quest for a cold Beer

Gali the 22th


In a nagar far, far away, two Jedi knights, Jatin and Rohit, have been on a mission to save the galaxy from the tyranny of the Sith. But after a long day of battling evil forces, they find themselves in need of a cold beer.

Jatin and Rohit make their way through the bustling streets of Coruscant, searching for a cantina that serves cold beer. Suddenly, they hear a disturbance in the Force. The Sith are trying to stop them from getting their beer.

The two sides clash in a fierce battle. Lightsabers clash, blaster fire rings out, and the air is thick with the smell of burning metal. The Jedi knights fight bravely, using their mastery of the Force to outmaneuver their foes.
